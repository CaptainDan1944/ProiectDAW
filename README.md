# ProiectDAW
University project website for an imaginary library.

Stormwind is one of the great human kingdoms in the Warcraft universe. It is a mostly medieval city, with thick stone walls, towers, a keep.
The city itself is split into 5 districts: the Trade District, the Old Town, the Dwarven District, the Cathedral Square and the Mage Quarters, alongisde the Keep and the Harbor. Our library is located in the Mage Quarters. As befitting its surroundings, our library specialises in more than just regular books. It contains magic texts, arcane scrolls, artifacts, city records and more. Our potential visitors can be distinguished by name, class (warrior, hunter, mage, warlock etc.), race (Warcraft is a world of many unique races: humans, orcs, blood elves, dwarves, night elves, tauren etc.), reputation with Stormwind and experience level. A book can either be lent for a price or, for some exemplaries, copies can be sold. Seminars also take place in the library weekly, where heroes of the same class gather to discuss knowledge relevant to their skillset. To obtain entry in the library and be able to buy / borrow books, as well as take part in seminars, a character must either have at least level 3 reputation in the service of Stormwind to earn the Librarian's Seal (special item), or have level 2 and pay a tax for access in the respective month.
